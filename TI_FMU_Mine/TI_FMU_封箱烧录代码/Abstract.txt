Blinky for MPS432 LaunchPad

Projects are configured for TI XDS-110ET emulator (on-board debug adapter), Keil ULINK2, ULINKpro and Segger J-Link.

This program runs at a CPU speed of 48 MHz.

For fastest operation and Serial Wire Viewer (SWV) use Keil ULINK2, ULINKpro, ULKINK-ME or J-Link.
For SWV operation, make sure Port: is set to SW and not JTAG.

This program uses TI Software Pack 2.2.0 or later.
